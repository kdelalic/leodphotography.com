My Story

My name is Leo Delalic. I am an artist, amongst many things, a professional photographer. My life mission is to travel the world and find hidden gems on the roads less travelled, to share with the public through my images. Over the years I have found that most places can be "picture perfect" but for some places that happens only for a brief moment when the light is just right. My passion is to chase that window of opportunity and capture it.

Landscape/travel photography is my passion but I do all types of photography from real estate to portrait photography.
